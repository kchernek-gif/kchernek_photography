Questions

What should I bring to the shoot?

Wardrobe per our planned looks (we’ll align in advance), a robe or cover-up between changes, water, and a snack. If you’re getting digitals, bring neutral basics: fitted jeans, a plain top, swim. We’ll discuss specifics during pre-shoot planning.

Do I need modeling experience?

No. First-time models book digitals all the time. I direct posing throughout the shoot, give you reference frames to look at on the back of the camera, and we go at your pace.

Can I bring someone with me?

Yes. A friend, parent, partner, or trusted representative is always welcome on set. Modeling is vulnerable work and you should feel comfortable.

When will I receive my photos?

Proof gallery within 7 days. Final retouched images within 2–3 weeks of the shoot. Rush delivery available if you have an agency deadline; let me know in advance.

What if I don’t like the final photos?

Selection happens collaboratively. I’ll send a proof gallery first; you choose which images get the final retouch. You won’t pay for retouching on images you don’t want to use.

Is hair and makeup included?

The Editorial Test package includes professional hair and makeup. The Digitals package does not. Digitals are intentionally minimal-styling so they show you as you are (which is what agencies want). I can recommend trusted HMU artists in DFW if you need referrals for any shoot.

Where do shoots take place?

Studio, outdoor DFW locations, or your chosen environment, depending on the look we’re planning. Studio rentals are factored into the package price; outdoor locations are typically free.

What if I need to reschedule?

Reschedules with 7+ days notice are accommodated at no charge. Cancellations within 7 days forfeit the deposit. Weather-related reschedules for outdoor shoots don’t count against this.
