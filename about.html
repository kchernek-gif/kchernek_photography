The part where I talk about myself.

Commercial fashion and portrait photographer based in Dallas–Fort Worth. I work with models, brands, and agencies that know what they want and need someone who can execute.

I shoot model portfolios (tests and digitals), brand visuals, and creative content for collaborators with a clear vision.

I’ve shot for brands and publications including Walmart, FloSports, Andi Bagus, and AvalTheBrand. My editorial work has been published across more than a dozen independent fashion magazines.

I’m based in Irving and shoot throughout DFW. I also run DFW Photowalks, a community group for local models and photographers building their books together.
