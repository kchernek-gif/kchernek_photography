Poolside swimwear editorial, Dallas, Texas, June 2024

The Project

A poolside swimwear shoot with model Lauren Fontan in Dallas, Texas, June 2024. Shot at The Village Country Club, this project used the cabana style seating to our advantage. The linen drapes added motion, and the blooming flowers added variety to the background.

Lauren's white cherry patterned swimsuit ended up being the perfect color compliment against the garden and drapes.

Outdoor poolside sessions in Dallas in June means working with the season's natural light rather than against it. Strong direct light, open sky. Embrace the sun kissed look, don't fight it.

As we shoot, i like to find ways for the model to interact with her wardrobe or environment. For this set, we chose to focus in on the colorful strings on the suit for some close up detail shots.
