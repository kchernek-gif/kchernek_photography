Cowgirl Swimwear

Commercial swimwear editorial, Caster House, Los Angeles, April 2026

Santorini in LA

I had the pleasure of working with Heloise at Caster House in Los Angeles, April 2026. Our stylist Monica paired a leopard print bikini with Tecovas boots and a cowboy hat. The western swimwear look gave us something really unique that you don't normally see in California or Texas.

Blending two different vibes can be a challenge, but the plain white walls let the tan colorset speak for itself. Caster House gave the shoot a backdrop that let us have endless soft bounced ligh; couldn't take a bad shot if we tried.

Caster House provided the structure: clean white architecture, strong horizontal lines, and good natural light throughout. The wardrobe mix of Tecovas boots, a cowboy hat, and a leopard print bikini drove the project's direction.

The shoot was built around a clear commercial brief: portfolio-grade imagery with a defined creative identity. That combination of a specific location and a distinct wardrobe direction is what gives this kind of work its range.
