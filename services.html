Services

Commercial fashion and lifestyle photography based in Dallas–Fort Worth. I work with models building their books, brands building their visuals, and creative collaborators with a clear vision.

Service categories

Portfolio Shoots

Planned, styled sessions for building your book. Considered concept, intentional lighting, professional hair and makeup. What the industry calls a test shoot or agency test.

Model Development (Tests + Digitals)

Agency-ready digitals and portfolio shoots. Built to help models develop their books and submit competitively.

Brand & Product Photography

Lookbook, campaign, and content work for brands. Project-based scoping, deliverables matched to your needs.

Events and Collaborations

Events, BTS, product, and collaborations that don’t fit the categories above. Describe the project and we’ll see if it’s a fit.

Process

Every shoot is planned, not improvised. We agree on the look, the location, and the shot list before the day starts. You end up with images you actually want to use.
