A portfolio shoot is a planned, styled session designed to build your book — not a client job, built entirely for you. Where digitals show who you are, a portfolio shoot shows what you can do: range, direction, creative concept. The industry calls this a test shoot or agency test. Sessions are built around shared mood references, a planned wardrobe, and a clear shot list. Output is fully retouched and agency-ready.
